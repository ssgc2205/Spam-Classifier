 This is data going back to 1896 that shows how the Dow Jones performed  during times when Mars was within 30 degrees of the lunar node. The data  contains the daily percentage changes of the Dow Jones since 1896. This  information was extrapolated from sources believed to be reliable  regarding stock market data.
